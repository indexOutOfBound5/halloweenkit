WARNINGS

This kit contains a coin cell battery, which if ingested can cause serious harm or even death. If ingested please bring your child to your nearest emergency room as soon as possible.

No part of this kit is edible.

Keep all parts away from children under 3.

Kids under 8 should have adult help.

TIPS

All parts of this kit are safe to handle gently.

This is a homemade kit. Some imperfections may be present. If some pieces are stubborn, a few light taps of a hammer should make them compliant.

DESCRIPTION

This kit contains the necessary components to make a small spooky light. You will make a circuit between a battery and an LED, and construct a box to be the base of the light. Finally, you will plug in your character to make it glow.
